# Staff Code of Conduct

## Professional Behavior
- Treat guests and coworkers with courtesy and respect.
- Maintain a positive and professional attitude at all times.

## Uniform & Grooming
- Employees must wear the assigned uniform during working hours.
- Uniforms should be clean and properly maintained.
- Personal hygiene and neat grooming are mandatory.

## Guest Privacy
- Never disclose guest information or room numbers to unauthorized individuals.
- Respect guest confidentiality at all times.

## Mobile Phone Usage
- Personal mobile phones should only be used during designated breaks.
- Phones must remain on silent mode while on duty.

## Workplace Ethics
- Theft, harassment, discrimination, and violence are strictly prohibited.
- Staff are expected to follow all hotel policies and local laws.

## Disciplinary Action
Failure to comply with the code of conduct may result in verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment.

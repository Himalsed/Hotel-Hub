# Restaurant Dining Policy

## Operating Hours
- Breakfast: 6:30 AM – 10:30 AM
- Lunch: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM
- Dinner: 6:00 PM – 10:30 PM

## Reservations
- Reservations are recommended during weekends and holidays.
- Tables will be held for 15 minutes after the reservation time.

## Dress Code
Smart casual attire is encouraged during dinner service.

## Outside Food
Outside food and beverages are not permitted except for baby food or medically required meals.

## Food Allergies
Guests should inform restaurant staff of any food allergies before ordering.

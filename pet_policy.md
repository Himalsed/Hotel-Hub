# Pet Policy

## Pet-Friendly Rooms
Only designated pet-friendly rooms allow pets.

## Allowed Pets
- Dogs and cats only.
- Maximum of two pets per room.
- Each pet must weigh less than 25 kg.

## Pet Fee
A cleaning fee of $30 per night per pet applies.

## Guest Responsibilities
- Pets must remain on a leash in public areas.
- Pets should not be left unattended in guest rooms.
- Owners are responsible for any damage caused by their pets.

## Restricted Areas
Pets are not allowed in:
- Restaurant
- Swimming Pool
- Spa
- Fitness Center

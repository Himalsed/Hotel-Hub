# Hotel Cancellation Policy

## Standard Reservations
- Guests may cancel their reservation free of charge up to **48 hours** before the scheduled check-in date.
- Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will incur a charge equal to one night's stay.

## Non-Refundable Bookings
- Reservations marked as Non-Refundable cannot be canceled or modified.
- No refunds will be provided regardless of the reason for cancellation.

## Group Reservations
- Reservations of five or more rooms require cancellation at least **14 days** before arrival.
- Failure to do so may result in a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the booking value.

## Early Departure
Guests who check out earlier than their confirmed departure date may be charged one additional night's room rate.

## Refund Processing
Eligible refunds are processed within 7–10 business days using the original payment method.

# Swimming Pool & Fitness Center Policy

## Swimming Pool Hours
Open daily from **7:00 AM to 9:00 PM**.

## Fitness Center Hours
Open 24 hours for registered hotel guests.

## Safety Rules
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.
- Running is prohibited around the pool.
- Glass containers are not allowed near the pool area.

## Hygiene
Guests must shower before entering the pool.

## Equipment Usage
Gym equipment should be wiped down after use.

## Towels
Complimentary towels are available at both the pool and fitness center.

## Liability
The hotel is not responsible for lost personal belongings in recreational areas.

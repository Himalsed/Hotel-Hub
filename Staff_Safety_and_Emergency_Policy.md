# Staff Safety and Emergency Policy

## Workplace Safety
- Follow all safety procedures and use equipment correctly.
- Report unsafe conditions immediately to the supervisor.

## Fire Safety
- Know the location of fire exits and fire extinguishers.
- Participate in mandatory fire drills and emergency training.

## Injury Reporting
- Report any workplace injury or accident immediately, regardless of severity.
- Complete an incident report before the end of the shift.

## Emergency Evacuation
- Assist guests in evacuating calmly during emergencies.
- Follow instructions from emergency response coordinators.

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Employees working in kitchens, maintenance, or housekeeping must wear the required PPE.
- Damaged or missing PPE should be reported immediately.

## First Aid
- First aid kits are available at designated locations throughout the property.
- Only trained personnel should administer first aid when appropriate.
